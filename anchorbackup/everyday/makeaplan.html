Make a Plan

It's better to always have a plan

Emergencies can happen suddenly. Maybe tomorrow, next week or next month. You can take steps now to help you prepare for an emergency and better protect yourself and loved ones.
Store emergency cash

ATMs might not work during a disaster, due to a blackout or surge withdrawing.


Have a go bag

Pack necessity items in a back pack; Such as important documents, water, snack bars, flashlight, back-up battery for iPhone, pen/paper, walkie-talkies, raincoat, and first-aid kit.


Where do you meet?

Incase of a disaster strikes, have a meeting place where your family and friends would meet.


Don't forget your Pets

Keep your pets in mind, they will need a go bag too.


Your location

A disaster can strike anywhere, anytime-when you are at the grocery story, gym or work. Be prepared to react and go.


Where do you stay?

Incase you cannot return home for a few days, have a place in mind where you can stay temporaty, until things calms down.
